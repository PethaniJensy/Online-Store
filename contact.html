Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery areas?

We currently deliver to most areas within 25 miles of our warehouse. Enter your ZIP
code at checkout to check availability.

How do you ensure freshness?

All produce is harvested within 24-48 hours of delivery and stored in climate-
controlled facilities. We use insulated packaging to maintain optimal temperature.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modify or cancelled up to 2 hours before your scheduled delivery
time. Contact customer service for assistance.

Do you offer organic certification?

Yes! All products marked as organic are certified by USDA-approved agencies.
Certificates are available upon request.
