About FreshMart

We're on a mission to make fresh, organic produce accessible to everyone. Founded in
2020, FreshMart connects local farms directly with your kitchen, ensuring the highest
quality and freshness in every delivery.

Our Story

FreshMart was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to fresh, healthy, and
sustainably grown food. Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, was a third-generation organic farmer
who saw the disconnect between farms and consumers.

After years of selling at farmers markets, Sarah realized that many people wanted fresh,
organic produce but couldn't always make it to the markest. That's when the idea for
FreshMart was born - bringing the farmer's market directly to your doorstep.

Today, we work with over 50 local farms across the region, supporting sustainable agriculture
while providing our customers with the freshest produce possible. Every vegetable and fruit
we deliver is picked at peak ripness and delivered within 24 hours.
